## Points of Interest
- Automatic teller machines (ATMs) found at: Mages' Guild entrance, Temple of Midgaard, Clerics' Guild entrance, Grunting Boar Inn reception
- The Grunting Boar Inn reception rents rooms — renting saves inventory and logs you out, useful for a clean session end
- The Huge Chain at the Road Crossing leads to a higher-level zone — avoid until stronger than level 1
- The Dump has an unexplored sewer entrance (`d`) — possible early dungeon
- A graveyard is accessible via an iron grate south of the concourse (unexplored)
